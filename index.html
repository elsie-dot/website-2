So why this website?

I hope that this website will help those gain more knowledge on the sport so they can speak freely about it without being judged by those who seem to know so much. This sport can be fairly judgemental towards other fans, many people may feel that they cannot express their love for Formula 1 in fear of being questioned about topics that they don't know about yet.

Formula 1 has recently become one of the most viewed sports since 2021 due to the on-track battles between drivers Max Verstappen and Lewis Hamilton. The sport has always been popular but it has received more media time recently attracting lots of new fans.

As someone who has only recently started watching and learning about Formula 1 since 2020, I understand how difficult it is to raise an opinion and then feel scrutinised because I don't know all of the deep facts on that topic.

To help, this website will include facts on:

  • What each tyre type means and when it is best to use them
  • What each part of the car does to help the team gain some points
  • Tips on each track every race weekend

I find these facts seem to be forgotten when i hear people talk about Formula 1 because some are more interested in the drivers, but it's not just the drivers causing the wins, but it's the car and the team as a whole that drives them forward and when it comes down to it.

I want my website to be an interesting and informative place for those who want to learn more and have a genuine interest in Formula 1, to learn, be ready and prepared to impress those who scrutinise you.
